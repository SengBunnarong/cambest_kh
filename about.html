About CamBest School

CamBest School was founded in 2026 with a simple but powerful vision to give every child in the community access to quality, affordable education in a safe and nurturing environment.

What started as a small classroom has grown into a full primary school serving many students across multiple grade levels, offering language programs, computer classes, and extra classes programs.

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and reach their full potential. That belief drives everything we do from how we teach to how we care for our students every day.

Our Teachers

Child Environment and Safety

We maintain a pristine environment where all learning items, tools, and equipment are strictly cleaned and completely safe for children to use.

National and International Kindergarten Curriculum

Children will receive a high-standard, comprehensive education that aligns with both national and international preschool benchmarks.
