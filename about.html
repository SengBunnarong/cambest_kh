About CamBest School

Learn about our story, our team, and our commitment to quality education.

Our Story

CamBest School was founded in 2026 with a simple but powerful vision — to give every child in the community access to quality, affordable education in a safe and nurturing environment.

What started as a small classroom has grown into a full primary school serving many students across multiple grade levels, offering language programs, computer classes, and extra class programs.

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and reach their full potential. That belief drives everything we do — from how we teach to how we care for our students every day.

Our Teachers

All teachers are thoroughly qualified and approved, demonstrating exceptional creativity and a strong commitment to child development.

Child Safety & Environment

We maintain a pristine environment where all learning items, tools, and equipment are strictly cleaned and completely safe for children to use.

National & International Curriculum

Children receive a high-standard, comprehensive education that aligns with both national and international preschool benchmarks.
